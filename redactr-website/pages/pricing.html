FAQ

Common Questions

Does Redactr see my employees' prompts?

No. Detection runs entirely on the employee's device. No prompt data is ever sent to Redactr's servers. We physically cannot see what your team types.

Which AI tools does it protect against?

Currently ChatGPT, Claude.ai, and Gemini.

What happens if I exceed my seat limit?

Each plan includes a fixed number of employee seats (5 on Starter, 25 on Professional, unlimited on Enterprise). Once you hit your limit, inviting another employee is blocked until you free up a seat or upgrade your plan.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No lock-in contracts. Cancel from your account dashboard and your plan stays active until the end of the billing period.
